FAQS

How much space is needed?

A standard 3kW system requires approximately 250-300 sq. ft. of shadow-free rooftop area.

How do I receive the ₹1.08L subsidy?

After installation and net-metering, the claim is verified on the portal. The amount is directly credited to your Aadhaar-linked bank account within 30 days.

What about cleaning and maintenance?

Panels should be cleaned with water every 15 days. We provide Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure peak performance year-round.
