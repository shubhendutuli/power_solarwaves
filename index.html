Your Questions, Answered.

Q: How do I get the ₹1,08,000 subsidy?

It is a combination of Central (₹78,000) and UP State (₹30,000) subsidies. We handle the entire application process on the national portal for you until the money hits your bank account.

Q: How much space is needed for a 3kW system?

You need approx 250-300 sq. ft of shadow-free rooftop area. We customize the structure based on your roof design.

Q: Can I run my AC on Solar?

Yes! With a Net-metering system, the solar power produced during the day offsets your AC usage at night, cutting your bill by up to 90%.
